# Vuqiro Design System

Vuqiro uses an original premium dark creator-first design.

## Brand

- Product: Vuqiro
- Legal owner: Diversa Solutions LLC
- Tagline: Discover creators. Support what you love.

## Visual direction

- Dark-first
- Premium
- Fast
- Global
- Creator-first
- Not a TikTok copy

## Palette

- Background: `#07070A`
- Surface: `#101016`
- Card: `#1C1C27`
- Text: `#F7F7FB`
- Muted: `#A6A6B8`
- Primary: `#7C3AED`
- Secondary: `#22D3EE`
- Success: `#22C55E`
- Warning: `#F59E0B`
- Danger: `#EF4444`

## UI rules

- Keep video central.
- Use creator CTA as a native product element, not an ad afterthought.
- Use clear locked-content states.
- Use premium cards and bottom sheets.
- Do not copy TikTok's exact action rail, icons, wording, colors or metadata.
