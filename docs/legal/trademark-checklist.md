# Trademark Checklist

Brand: Vuqiro  
Company: Diversa Solutions LLC

Before launch:

- Search USPTO.
- Search EUIPO.
- Search WIPO Global Brand Database.
- Search Apple App Store.
- Search Google Play Store.
- Search domain availability.
- Search social handles.
- Check similar names visually/phonetically.
- Check relevant classes with trademark attorney, including software, social networking, video, entertainment, creator marketplace and payments.
- Do not claim trademark registration unless actually filed/registered.
