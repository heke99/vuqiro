# Age Rating & Safety

> These are product and implementation outlines, not final legal advice.
> Final legal documents must be reviewed by a qualified attorney before launch.

Owner: Diversa Solutions LLC · Product: Vuqiro

## Target age rating

- **Apple**: 17+ (frequent/intense UGC potential, unrestricted web-adjacent
  content categories). Could be argued down to 12+ once moderation staffing
  and filtering maturity are demonstrated; launch conservatively at 17+.
- **Google Play**: complete the IARC questionnaire truthfully as a UGC
  platform with moderation → typically Teen/Mature 17+ depending on region.

## Minimum user age

- 13+ globally (16+ where local law requires for data processing without
  parental consent, e.g. some EU states). Age gate at signup (date-of-birth
  screen) is required before launch — currently the terms acceptance asserts
  age; a DOB field should be added when final legal text is adopted.

## UGC safety controls (store requirements — implemented)

- Report video / comment / profile with 10 reason categories.
- Block users (content hidden bidirectionally).
- Moderation queue with remove/limit/age-restrict/suspend/ban + appeals.
- Minor-safety reports auto-escalate to critical priority.
- Age-restricted content shows an 18+ interstitial and is downranked.
- Community Guidelines accessible in-app without login barriers.
- Account deletion in-app.

## Content the rating depends on

- No sexually explicit content permitted (Community Guidelines).
- Violence/profanity possible in UGC → declared in rating questionnaires.
- Gambling: none. Contests: none at launch.
- "Digital purchases" declared: coins, subscriptions, boosts.

## Owner actions before submission

- Complete Apple age-rating questionnaire in App Store Connect.
- Complete Google IARC questionnaire in Play Console.
- Add DOB age gate to signup (tracked for launch).
