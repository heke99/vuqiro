# App Store / Google Play Compliance Foundation

## UGC requirements

- Report content
- Block users
- Moderation queue
- Remove content
- Ban/suspend users
- Contact/support channel
- Community guidelines

## Payment requirements

- Digital content in app uses Apple IAP / Google Play Billing / RevenueCat.
- Subscriptions show terms and renewal info.
- One-time coin purchases use store products.
- Restore purchases must be available.
- Backend verifies entitlements.

## Creator content

- Clearly show what requires purchase.
- Locked content labels.
- Age rating must reflect highest available creator content.
- Moderation covers creator content.

## Privacy

- Privacy policy required.
- Data deletion flow required.
- Account deletion flow required.
